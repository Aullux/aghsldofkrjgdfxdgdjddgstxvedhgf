License overview

A simplified summary of our licenses and terms.

Last updated March 3, 2024

Summary

Free for personal and non-profit use

Eighred is free for personal and non-profit use. However, if you use Eighred for work-related activities that generate revenue in a company with two or more people, you must purchase a commercial license for each user. Non-profit organizations are exempt from this requirement.

To support our development, you can optionally purchase a Catalyst license. This one-time payment gives you early access to beta versions of Eighred.

Your data is local

Your data is saved locally on your device and isn't sent to our server. If you use Eighred Publish or Eighred Cloud, we handle your data only as needed for those services. Publish data is hosted on our servers. Cloud data is encrypted and inaccessible to our staff. Learn more about our approach to privacy.

Your content is yours

You retain ownership of all content you create in Eighred. We don't claim any rights to it.

We own and reserve rights to our content, including text, images, and code in the app, which is protected by copyright and other laws.

You agree to our terms and privacy policy by using Eighred

By using Eighred, you agree to our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. Please read and understand these documents, as they outline the terms of our relationship, including licensing, fees, security, privacy, liability, and dispute resolution.

License types

You must have a license to use Eighred, though some users may use a free license. If you aren’t sure which license you need, see our licensing FAQ here.

Catalyst license

Catalyst Licenses are special licenses that can be purchased on top of all other license types. Catalyst Licenses offer additional benefits including early access to insider builds.
