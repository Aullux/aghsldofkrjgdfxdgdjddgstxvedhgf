FAQ

Frequently asked questions about pricing

Can I buy Eighred for someone else?

Yes! You can purchase Eighred Credit for friends, family, and coworkers. This credit can be used to pay for any Eighred license or service. To get started, create an account and buy credit. You can then send it to the recipient via email or a shareable link. Learn more.

What is your refund policy?

We provide full refunds within 7 days of purchase for commercial licenses, Eighred Publish, and Eighred Cloud — no questions asked. However, please note that Catalyst licenses and Eighred Credit are non-refundable. Learn more.

Do you store, access, or process user data?

Eighred is a private and secure space for your thoughts. You can use our apps without sharing any personal information, and your data is stored locally on your device, making it inaccessible to us.

If you choose to use Eighred Cloud, your data is secured with AES‑256 end-to-end encryption, preventing us from reading it. Learn more.

Do you offer discounts for education and nonprofits?

Yes, we do! Students, faculty members, and nonprofit employees are eligible for a 25% discount on Eighred Cloud. We do not offer any other discounts or promotions. Learn more.

What benefits does the Catalyst license grant?

By becoming a Catalyst member, you'll receive early access to beta versions of Eighred, as well as special badges within the community. Catalyst plays a vital role in helping Eighred remain 100% user-supported, free from investor influence that could compromise our values.
