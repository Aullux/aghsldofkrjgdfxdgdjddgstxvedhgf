Features

Safe and secure

Eighred Cloud works in the background to keep your data effortlessly and privately.

End-to-end encryption
Your data is automatically secured using AES‑256, the strongest encryption standard. With Eighred Cloud you're always in total control of your data. Learn more →
Requesting your account data
Download and export a report of your account data. This report does not include any messages or media
Cross-platform apps
Eighred is available on all major platforms, so you can Cloud data to any device. Seamlessly work across Mac, Windows, Linux, iOS and Android.
Offline chat
Eighred is designed to work even without an internet connection.It is used to send messages from your phone to your friends using Bluetooth from a distance of 100 mt. / 330 ft. (a whole football field!).

Fine-grained Cloud control
